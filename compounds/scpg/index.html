(Sanskrit Compound Paraphrase Generator)

 
 
 

Introduction

   The Paraphrase generator is final step of Sanskrit compound processor. This module generates the paraphrase autometically from a tagged compound word. For instance :-

<<Sumitrā-ānanda>T6-vardhanaḥ>T6

is paraphrased as

Sumitrāyāḥ ānandaḥ = Sumitrānandaḥ

Sumitrānandasya vardhanaḥ = Sumitrānanda- vardhanaḥ
